Marking system (All equal weighting)

  • Short tests: at the end of each lesson, a 10-minute test consisting of 4 questions will be done without any documents.
  • Project: details can be found here.
  • Exam: one hour final exam (paper, pencil) with your own notes on one A4 sheet (double sided).
